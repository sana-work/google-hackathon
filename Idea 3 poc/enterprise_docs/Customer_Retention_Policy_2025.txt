1. PURPOSE & SCOPE
   This policy establishes the enterprise-wide framework for proactive customer
   retention, early churn detection, and structured intervention protocols.
   It applies to all customer-facing teams across SaaS, Telecom, Banking,
   Insurance, and E-commerce verticals.

2. STRATEGIC CONTEXT
   Customer acquisition costs 5-7x more than retention. A 5% improvement in
   retention rates can increase profits by 25-95% (Harvard Business Review).
   The organization must shift from reactive (responding to cancellations) to
   proactive (detecting disengagement before churn occurs).

3. SILENT CHURN FRAMEWORK
   3.1 Definition: "Silent churn" occurs when a customer disengages without
       filing complaints, responding to surveys, or making explicit cancellation
       requests. Research shows 60-70% of total churn is silent.
   3.2 Detection Window: All retention interventions must target a 30-60 day
       window BEFORE predicted churn date. Interventions within 7 days of
       churn have <15% success rate.
   3.3 Multi-Channel Signal Monitoring: Teams must monitor FOUR behavioral
       channels simultaneously:
       - Platform Usage (login frequency, feature depth, session duration)
       - Communication (email opens, notification responses, newsletter clicks)
       - Support (ticket creation, survey response, FAQ visits)
       - Transactions (purchase frequency, payment timeliness, plan changes)

4. SILENT RISK SCORE (SRS)
   4.1 Every customer account must have a computed Silent Risk Score (0-100):
       - 0-40: LOW RISK — Normal engagement, monitor only
       - 41-70: MEDIUM RISK — Declining engagement, proactive outreach required
       - 71-100: HIGH RISK — Critical disengagement, immediate escalation
   4.2 The SRS is calculated using weighted behavioral signals:
       SRS = w1(Usage_Decline) + w2(Communication_Silence) +
             w3(Support_Absence) + w4(Transaction_Decay)
       Where weights are calibrated quarterly based on historical churn outcomes.
   4.3 Score refresh frequency: Daily for HIGH RISK, Weekly for MEDIUM, Monthly
       for LOW RISK accounts.

5. MANDATORY INTERVENTION PROTOCOL
   5.1 LOW RISK (SRS 0-40):
       - Automated personalized re-engagement email within 48 hours
       - Content must highlight features the customer hasn't explored
       - No human intervention required unless score increases
   5.2 MEDIUM RISK (SRS 41-70):
       - Customer Success Manager (CSM) must initiate proactive outreach
         within 24 hours of score breach
       - Outreach script must reference specific behavioral changes observed
       - Offer priority support queue access
       - Document intervention in CRM within 4 hours of contact
   5.3 HIGH RISK (SRS 71-100):
       - Immediate escalation to Relationship Manager / Account Director
       - Full context brief auto-generated including: behavioral timeline,
         open tickets, payment history, and recommended retention offer
       - Manager must make personal contact within 12 hours
       - Retention budget authorization: up to 20% of annual contract value
       - HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP REQUIRED: All high-risk interventions must have
         human approval before customer contact

6. REPORTING & ACCOUNTABILITY
   6.1 Weekly Retention Dashboard: All teams must review cohort churn metrics
   6.2 Monthly Retention Board: Executive review of intervention effectiveness
   6.3 KPIs: Net Revenue Retention (NRR), Intervention Success Rate,
       Time-to-Intervention, Customer Health Score trend

7. DATA GOVERNANCE
   7.1 All behavioral data used for churn prediction must be anonymized
   7.2 No PII shall be included in prediction models
   7.3 Customer communication preferences and opt-outs must be respected
   7.4 Data retention: Behavioral signals retained for 24 months, then purged
