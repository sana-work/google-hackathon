1 Problem Statement

The most dangerous customer attrition is silent disengagement. These customers don't complain, don't leave reviews, don't respond to surveys. They simply fade away.

⚠ Blind Spot: Only 4% of unhappy customers complain. Survey response rates are 5-15%. That means 60-70% of churning customers show none of the traditional churn signals. By the time you notice, they're gone.
Metric Impact
Acquisition vs retention cost Acquiring is 5-7x more expensive
Impact of 5% churn reduction 25-95% profit increase (HBR)
Silent churn proportion 60-70% of total churn
Detection with current tools <30% of silent churners found in time

2 Solution

The Silent Escalation Predictor treats the absence of engagement as the most powerful signal — fusing multi-channel behavioral data into a composite risk score and triggering personalized interventions.

Multi-Channel Behavioral Fusion

Channel Signals Silence Indicators
📱 Platform Usage Logins, feature depth, sessions Declining logins, shallow sessions
📧 Communication Email opens, notifications, newsletters Ignored emails, unopened content
🎫 Support Tickets, surveys, FAQ visits No tickets (disengaged, not happy)
💳 Transactions Purchases, payments, plan changes Declining frequency, late payments
