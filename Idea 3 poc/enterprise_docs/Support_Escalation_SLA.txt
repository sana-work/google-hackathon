DOCUMENT: SUPPORT ESCALATION SLA — RETENTION PRIORITY
CLASSIFICATION: INTERNAL — OPERATIONS
EFFECTIVE DATE: February 1, 2025
OWNER: VP Customer Support
================================================================================

1. PURPOSE
   Defines the Service Level Agreements for support tickets involving
   customers flagged by the Silent Escalation Predictor as at-risk.

2. PRIORITY CLASSIFICATION
   When the Silent Escalation Predictor flags a customer as MEDIUM or
   HIGH RISK, all their open and future support tickets automatically
   receive elevated priority.

   | Customer Risk | Ticket Priority | First Response SLA | Resolution SLA  |
   |---------------|-----------------|--------------------|-----------------| 
   | LOW (0-40)    | Standard (P3)   | 24 hours           | 5 business days |
   | MEDIUM (41-70)| Elevated (P2)   | 4 hours            | 2 business days |
   | HIGH (71-100) | Critical (P1)   | 1 hour             | 4 hours         |

3. ESCALATION WORKFLOW FOR AT-RISK CUSTOMERS
   3.1 When a HIGH RISK customer creates a ticket:
       - Auto-assign to senior support engineer
       - Notify Customer Success Manager immediately
       - Add "RETENTION_PRIORITY" tag to ticket
       - Include behavioral context in ticket notes
   3.2 When a HIGH RISK customer has an open ticket >48 hours:
       - Auto-escalate to Support Director
       - Trigger proactive outreach: "We're working on your issue"
       - Offer interim workaround or credit

4. PROACTIVE SUPPORT FOR SILENT CUSTOMERS
   4.1 Customers with SRS >60 AND zero tickets in 90 days:
       - Support team must initiate "Health Check" outreach
       - Template: "We noticed you might be experiencing [known issue].
         We'd like to proactively help resolve any concerns."
   4.2 This proactive outreach counts as an intervention and must be
       logged in the retention tracking system.

5. METRICS & REPORTING
   - Track SLA compliance for at-risk vs. standard customers
   - Measure correlation between support resolution speed and churn
   - Report to Retention Board monthly
   - Target: 95% SLA compliance for P1/P2 tickets
