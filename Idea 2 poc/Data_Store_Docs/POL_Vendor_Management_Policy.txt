VERSION: 1.4

OWNER: Chief Procurement Officer (CPO) / Vendor Risk Management Team
APPLICABILITY: All third-party vendors, contractors, sub-processors, and technology partners

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 1 — VENDOR ONBOARDING AND ASSESSMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Vendor Risk Assessment: All new vendors handling company or customer data must undergo a risk assessment covering: data handling practices, security certifications (SOC2, ISO27001), financial stability, and jurisdictional risk.

1.2 Data Processing Agreement (DPA): A signed DPA is mandatory before any vendor receives access to personal data. The DPA must include: data processing purposes, data categories, retention limits, breach notification obligations, and audit rights.

1.3 Vendor Classification:
    (a) CRITICAL: Vendors processing sensitive personal data or operating critical infrastructure (e.g., cloud providers, payment processors)
    (b) HIGH: Vendors with access to business-sensitive data (e.g., analytics providers, CRM platforms)
    (c) STANDARD: Vendors with limited data access (e.g., office supplies, facility management)

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2 — ONGOING MONITORING
═══════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Annual Reviews: CRITICAL vendors undergo annual security assessments including SOC2 report review and questionnaire-based assessment.

2.2 Audit Rights: The organization reserves the right to audit CRITICAL vendors with 30 days advance notice.

2.3 Sub-Processor Notification: Vendors must notify us of any new sub-processors 30 days in advance of engagement. The organization has right to object.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3 — DATA LOCATION AND SOVEREIGNTY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Vendors must process data only in regions explicitly approved in the contract.
3.2 Any vendor infrastructure relocation requires 60 days advance notice and written approval.
3.3 Current approved vendor data locations: India (Mumbai, Chennai), EU (Frankfurt), US (Virginia), Singapore (for DR only).
    — NOTE: Singapore is currently approved for disaster recovery for non-financial data only.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4 — BREACH AND INCIDENT REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Vendors must report any data breach or security incident to the organization's CISO within 48 hours of detection.
4.2 Vendors must cooperate fully in incident investigations and provide all requested logs and access within 72 hours.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5 — CONTRACT TERMINATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Upon termination, vendors must return all data in a portable format within 30 days.
5.2 Vendors must certify complete data deletion within 60 days of termination.
5.3 Post-termination audit rights extend for 12 months.
