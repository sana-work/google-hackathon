1. INCIDENT SUMMARY
   In Q3 2024, the organization lost 23 enterprise accounts (combined ACV
   ₹8.7 Cr) with ZERO advance warning from traditional monitoring systems.
   Post-exit surveys revealed these customers had been disengaging for
   3-6 months before final departure.

2. TIMELINE OF EVENTS — CASE STUDY: ACME FINTECH (ACV ₹42L)

   Month 1 (April 2024):
   - Login frequency: 15/week (normal)
   - Email engagement: 68% open rate (normal)
   - Support tickets: 2 tickets, resolved in 3 days
   - Payments: On time

   Month 2 (May 2024):
   - Login frequency: 12/week (↓20% — FIRST SIGNAL, MISSED)
   - Email engagement: 45% open rate (↓34%)
   - Support tickets: 1 ticket, resolved in 5 days
   - Payments: On time

   Month 3 (June 2024):
   - Login frequency: 6/week (↓60% — CRITICAL SIGNAL, MISSED)
   - Email engagement: 20% open rate (↓71%)
   - Support tickets: 0 — customer stopped engaging
   - Payments: On time but auto-debit

   Month 4 (July 2024):
   - Login frequency: 2/week (↓87%)
   - Email engagement: 0% — all emails ignored
   - Support tickets: 0
   - Payments: Auto-renewal cancelled (FIRST DETECTED SIGNAL)

   Month 5 (August 2024):
   - Customer departed. No response to retention outreach.
   - Exit survey: "We didn't feel supported. We found an alternative
     that better fits our needs. Nobody reached out proactively."

3. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
   3.1 No multi-channel behavioral monitoring in place
   3.2 Support team only tracked ticket volume, not absence patterns
   3.3 CSM team relied on quarterly check-ins (too infrequent)
   3.4 Payment cancellation was the ONLY signal detected — too late
   3.5 No system to calculate composite risk from combined decline signals

4. KEY LEARNINGS
   L1: Silence is the loudest signal — zero complaints doesn't mean happy
   L2: Multi-channel fusion would have detected risk at Month 2 (SRS ~55)
   L3: Proactive outreach at Month 2 had estimated 44% save probability
   L4: By Month 4, save probability dropped to <10%
   L5: The 30-60 day intervention window is critical and non-negotiable

5. IMPACT
   - Total enterprise churn in Q3: ₹8.7 Cr ACV
   - Estimated saveable with early detection: ₹3.5-5.2 Cr (40-60%)
   - Competitive displacement identified in 15 of 23 cases
   - Brand reputation impact: 3 negative Gartner Peer Insights reviews

6. CORRECTIVE ACTIONS
   CA-1: Build and deploy Silent Escalation Predictor (this project)
   CA-2: Implement daily multi-channel behavioral scoring
   CA-3: Reduce CSM check-in cadence to monthly for enterprise accounts
   CA-4: Establish proactive support protocol for silent customers
   CA-5: Executive-sponsored retention review board (monthly)
