GLOBEX CORPORATION — HUMAN RESOURCES LEAVE POLICY v3.2
Document ID: HR-POL-003 | Effective: January 1, 2026
Classification: Internal | Owner: VP Human Resources
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. PRIVILEGE LEAVE (PL)
────────────────────────
1.1 All full-time employees are entitled to a maximum of 30 days Privilege Leave per calendar year.
1.2 PL is credited on January 1st each year. Unused PL can be carried forward up to a maximum of 15 days.
1.3 Any PL carried forward beyond 15 days will be automatically encashed at the employee's basic salary rate.
1.4 PL requests of 5 or more consecutive days require manager approval at least 14 days in advance.
1.5 PL requests during Q4 (October-December) are subject to business-critical project review and may be denied if the employee is on a client-deliverable project.

2. SICK LEAVE (SL)
────────────────────
2.1 Employees are entitled to 12 days of Sick Leave per year.
2.2 Sick Leave exceeding 3 consecutive days requires a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner.
2.3 Sick Leave cannot be encashed and lapses on December 31st each year.
2.4 Employees on Long-Term Disability (LTD) must apply through the HR Benefits Portal within 7 days of the medical event.

3. MATERNITY & PATERNITY LEAVE
──────────────────────────────
3.1 Maternity Leave: 26 weeks (for first two children), 12 weeks (for third child onwards), as per the Maternity Benefit Act 2017.
3.2 Paternity Leave: 15 working days, to be availed within 6 months of the child's birth or adoption.
3.3 Adoption Leave: 12 weeks for primary caregiver, 15 days for secondary caregiver.

4. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE
─────────────────────
4.1 Up to 5 working days for immediate family (spouse, children, parents, siblings).
4.2 Up to 3 working days for extended family (grandparents, in-laws, uncles, aunts).
4.3 Must be availed within 30 days of the event.

5. COMPENSATORY OFF
────────────────────
5.1 Employees working on holidays or weekends are entitled to Compensatory Off (CO) on a 1:1 basis.
5.2 CO must be availed within 30 days of accrual.
5.3 CO requests require prior approval from the reporting manager and must be logged in the HRMS system within 48 hours of the extra work day.

6. LEAVE WITHOUT PAY (LWP)
───────────────────────────
6.1 LWP may be granted when all other leave balances are exhausted.
6.2 LWP exceeding 30 days in a calendar year triggers a review of employment status by the HR Governance Committee.
6.3 LWP days are deducted from the employee's tenure for the purpose of annual increment and promotion eligibility calculations.

7. LEAVE APPROVAL HIERARCHY
────────────────────────────
7.1 Up to 3 days: Reporting Manager
7.2 4-10 days: Reporting Manager + Department Head
7.3 11+ days: Reporting Manager + Department Head + HR Business Partner
7.4 All leave requests must be submitted through the Globex HRMS Portal (hrms.globex-internal.com).

8. HOLIDAY CALENDAR 2026
─────────────────────────
Globex Corporation observes 12 public holidays per year. The list is published in the HRMS Portal by November 30th of the preceding year. Employees may additionally select 2 Restricted Holidays from a list of 8 options.
