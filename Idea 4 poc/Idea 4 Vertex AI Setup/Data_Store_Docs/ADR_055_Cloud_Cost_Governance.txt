DOCUMENT TYPE: Architectural Decision Record (ADR)
ID: ADR-055
DATE: 2025-10-01
TOPIC: Cloud Cost Governance and FinOps Standards
STATUS: Accepted

CONTEXT:
Cloud infrastructure costs increased 340% year-over-year without proportional business growth. Root cause analysis identified: engineers provisioning oversized instances, no auto-scaling caps, GPU usage for non-ML workloads, forgotten development environments running 24/7, and no cost accountability at the team level. CFO escalated this to the CTO as a P1 operational issue.

DECISION:
All cloud resource provisioning MUST follow the cost governance standards outlined below. Team leads are accountable for their team's cloud spend.

REQUIREMENTS:

1. RESOURCE LABELING:
   All cloud resources (VMs, GKE clusters, Cloud SQL, BigQuery datasets, Pub/Sub topics, Cloud Storage buckets) MUST have the following labels:
   - team: [owning team name]
   - environment: [production | staging | development]
   - cost-center: [finance cost center code]
   - project: [project or product name]
   Resources without labels will be flagged for deletion after 7 days.

2. COMPUTE INSTANCE GOVERNANCE:
   - Development environments MUST use preemptible/spot instances. On-demand instances for dev/test are prohibited.
   - Production auto-scaling MUST have a maximum instance cap. Unlimited auto-scaling is prohibited.
   - GPU instances (A100, T4, L4) require FinOps Team approval and are permitted ONLY for ML model training and inference workloads.
   - Instance types larger than n2-standard-8 require team lead approval with documented justification.

3. DEVELOPMENT ENVIRONMENT LIFECYCLE:
   - All non-production environments (dev, staging, sandbox) MUST auto-shutdown outside business hours (auto-stop at 20:00, auto-start at 08:00 local time).
   - Development environments unused for 14 days are automatically flagged for deletion.
   - Each team is allocated a maximum monthly dev/staging budget. Alerts fire at 80% and 100%.

4. STORAGE LIFECYCLE:
   - All Cloud Storage buckets MUST have lifecycle policies. Data older than 90 days must transition to Nearline or Coldline storage.
   - BigQuery datasets must use partition expiration. Tables without partition expiration are flagged.

5. COST REVIEWS:
   - Monthly cost review meetings per team, led by team leads.
   - Any single resource costing more than $1,000/month requires documented justification.

ENFORCEMENT:
Non-compliant resources will receive automated warnings at Day 1, Day 3, and Day 7. After Day 7, non-compliant resources in non-production environments are automatically stopped.

- Global Security Policy v4, Section 5 (infrastructure rules still apply even with cost optimization)
