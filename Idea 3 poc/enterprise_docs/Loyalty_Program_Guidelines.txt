1. PURPOSE
   Defines how the loyalty program interfaces with the Silent Escalation
   Predictor to deliver targeted retention offers to at-risk customers.

2. LOYALTY TIERS & RETENTION PRIVILEGES

   Tier       | Qualification     | Retention Offer Cap | Exclusive Benefits
   -----------|-------------------|---------------------|-------------------------
   Bronze     | 0-12 months       | 5% discount         | Priority email support
   Silver     | 12-24 months      | 10% discount        | Free feature upgrade (30d)
   Gold       | 24-36 months      | 15% discount        | Dedicated CSM + training
   Platinum   | 36+ months        | 20% discount        | Executive sponsor + roadmap input

3. AUTOMATED LOYALTY INTERVENTIONS

   3.1 Milestone Recognition (Proactive):
       - Auto-send celebration at 6, 12, 24, 36 month anniversaries
       - Include usage statistics and value delivered
       - Personalized thank-you from account executive

   3.2 At-Risk Loyalty Activation:
       When SRS exceeds 40, the loyalty system auto-generates:
       - Tier-appropriate discount offer
       - Exclusive early access to upcoming features
       - Invitation to customer advisory board (Gold/Platinum only)
       - Personalized ROI report showing delivered value

4. LOYALTY POINTS FOR RE-ENGAGEMENT
   - Complete a product survey: 500 points
   - Attend a webinar: 300 points
   - Try a new feature: 200 points
   - Provide a testimonial: 1000 points
   - Refer another customer: 2000 points

5. RESTRICTIONS
   - Loyalty offers cannot stack with existing contractual discounts
   - Maximum 1 retention loyalty offer per 90-day period
   - Platinum customers with SRS >80 bypass loyalty system → direct
     executive intervention required
