1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
   Q4 2025 saw total customer churn of 8.2% across all verticals, up from
   7.1% in Q3 2025. Critically, 67% of churned customers exhibited NO
   traditional warning signals (complaints, escalations, negative surveys)
   before departure — reinforcing the urgency of the Silent Escalation
   Predictor initiative.

3. ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS — TOP 5 SILENT CHURN DRIVERS

   3.1 Feature Abandonment (28% of cases)
       Customers who stopped using 3+ features within 30 days had 4.2x
       higher churn probability. Most common in SaaS (reporting, integrations).

   3.2 Communication Fatigue (22% of cases)
       Customers receiving >8 emails/week showed 3.1x higher email ignore
       rates, correlating with 2.8x churn probability increase.

   3.3 Unresolved Support Debt (18% of cases)
       Customers with tickets open >14 days who STOPPED following up
       (rather than escalating) churned at 5.1x the baseline rate.

   3.4 Payment Pattern Shift (17% of cases)
       First-time late payment in accounts with 12+ months tenure was the
       strongest single predictor of churn within 60 days (OR = 6.3).

   3.5 Competitor Evaluation (15% of cases)
       Detected via reduced feature depth + login pattern changes (short,
       targeted sessions vs. broad exploration). Hard to detect without
       multi-channel fusion.

   Interventions made within 48 hours of risk score breach had 2.3x
   higher success rate than those delayed beyond 7 days.

5. COHORT INSIGHTS

   5.1 Enterprise SaaS: Customers show 3x higher silent churn risk after
       onboarding month 4 — the "feature discovery cliff"
   5.2 Telecom: Weekend-only users who shift to zero weekend usage churn
       within 21 days (92% accuracy)
   5.3 Banking: Transaction frequency dropping below 2x/month after
       averaging 8x/month signals imminent account closure
   5.4 E-commerce: Cart abandonment rate increasing from <20% to >60%
       over 30 days predicts churn with 78% accuracy

6. RECOMMENDATIONS
   - Deploy Silent Escalation Predictor across all verticals by Q2 2026
   - Reduce intervention response time target from 48h to 24h
   - Implement real-time scoring (currently batch-daily)
   - Add in-app behavioral triggers alongside email/phone channels
