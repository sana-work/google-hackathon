DOCUMENT TYPE: Regulatory Circular
REGULATION: Reserve Bank of India — Data Localization Directive for Payment System Operators
CIRCULAR NUMBER: RBI/2025-26/48 / DPSS.CO.OD No. 2785/06.08.005/2025-26
DATE OF ISSUANCE: February 1, 2026
EFFECTIVE DATE: August 1, 2026 (180-day compliance window)
JURISDICTION: Republic of India
APPLICABILITY: All Payment System Operators (PSOs), Payment Aggregators (PAs), Banks, NBFCs, and their technology service providers operating in India

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 1 — DATA STORAGE REQUIREMENTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Complete Data Localization: ALL payment data — including full end-to-end transaction data, payment credentials, customer financial profiles, KYC records, and transaction metadata — MUST be stored exclusively within the territory of India in data centers physically located in India.

1.2 Definition of Payment Data: For the purposes of this circular, "payment data" includes:
    (a) Customer identifying information (name, mobile number, email, Aadhaar/PAN linked to payment accounts)
    (b) Payment credentials (card numbers, UPI VPAs, bank account numbers, wallet IDs)
    (c) Transaction data (amount, date, merchant details, payment status, settlement records)
    (d) KYC and verification records associated with payment accounts

1.3 No Foreign Mirrors: Payment data SHALL NOT be mirrored, replicated, or backed up to data centers outside India, including for disaster recovery purposes. Disaster recovery sites must also be located within India.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2 — PROCESSING RESTRICTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Domestic Processing Only: All processing of payment data — including real-time transaction processing, fraud detection, analytics, and reporting — MUST occur within India.

2.2 Foreign Processing Exception: The ONLY permitted exception is for cross-border transactions where the foreign leg of the transaction requires data to be temporarily shared with the foreign counterpart. In such cases:
    (a) Only the minimum data necessary for transaction completion may be shared
    (b) Data must be deleted from the foreign system within 24 hours of transaction settlement
    (c) A log of all foreign data shares must be maintained and reported to RBI quarterly

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3 — THIRD-PARTY AND VENDOR OBLIGATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Vendor Compliance: All third-party vendors, cloud service providers, and technology partners who store or process payment data on behalf of regulated entities MUST ensure their infrastructure meets the data localization requirements of this circular.

3.2 Contractual Obligations: Regulated entities MUST include explicit data localization clauses in ALL vendor and third-party contracts. Existing contracts must be amended within the compliance window.

3.3 Sub-Processor Restrictions: Third-party processors SHALL NOT engage sub-processors located outside India for any activity involving payment data without prior written approval from RBI.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4 — AUDIT AND REPORTING
═══════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 Annual Compliance Audit: Every regulated entity MUST submit an annual System Audit Report (SAR) to RBI confirming complete data localization compliance. The SAR must be prepared by a CERT-IN empanelled auditor.

4.2 Board-Level Reporting: The board of directors of every regulated entity must receive a quarterly report on data localization compliance status.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5 — PENALTIES AND ENFORCEMENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Non-compliance will attract:
    (a) Monetary penalty of up to ₹5 Crore per month of violation
    (b) Restriction or revocation of payment system authorization
    (c) Direction to cease and desist from processing transactions until compliance is achieved

5.2 Entities must report any data localization breach to RBI within 72 hours of discovery.
