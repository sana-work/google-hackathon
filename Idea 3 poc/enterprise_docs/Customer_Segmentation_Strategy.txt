1. PURPOSE
   Defines the customer segmentation model used for tailored retention
   strategies and cohort-level churn analysis.

3. INDUSTRY VERTICAL SEGMENTS

   Segment         | Churn Profile                    | Key Retention Levers
   ----------------|----------------------------------|----------------------------
   BFSI            | Low churn, high value per exit   | Compliance assurance, security
   Telecom         | High volume churn, price-sensitive| Loyalty rewards, bundle offers
   SaaS/Tech       | Feature-driven, competitive      | Product roadmap, integrations
   Retail/E-comm   | Seasonal patterns, impulse-driven | Personalization, recommendations
   Insurance       | Renewal-cycle driven             | Pre-renewal engagement
   Healthcare      | Compliance-locked, sticky        | Data migration complexity

4. BEHAVIORAL SEGMENTS (Cross-cutting)

   4.1 Power Users (15% of base)
       - High engagement across all channels
       - Low churn risk BUT high impact if lost
       - Retention focus: Feature exclusivity, advisory board membership

   4.2 Steady State (55% of base)
       - Consistent, moderate engagement
       - Baseline churn risk
       - Retention focus: Value reinforcement, ROI reports

   4.3 Declining Engagers (20% of base)
       - Visible decline in 1-2 channels
       - Medium churn risk (SRS 41-70)
       - Retention focus: Proactive CSM outreach, feature education

   4.4 Silent Faders (10% of base)
       - Multi-channel decline, no complaints
       - HIGH churn risk (SRS 71-100)
       - Retention focus: Immediate escalation, executive intervention
       - THIS IS THE PRIMARY TARGET SEGMENT FOR THE PREDICTOR

5. COHORT ANALYSIS INSIGHTS (Q4 2025)

   5.1 Enterprise SaaS customers show 3x higher silent churn risk after
       onboarding month 4 ("feature discovery cliff")
   5.2 Telecom customers with weekend-only usage who shift to zero weekend
       usage churn within 21 days (92% accuracy)
   5.3 Banking customers: Transaction frequency below 2x/month after
       averaging 8x/month signals closure within 30 days
   5.4 E-commerce: Cart abandonment increasing from <20% to >60% over
       30 days predicts churn with 78% accuracy
   5.5 Insurance: Customers not opening renewal reminder emails 60 days
       before expiry have 5.8x higher lapse rate
