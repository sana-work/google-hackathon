CONTRACT: Cloud Infrastructure Services Agreement
VENDOR: Acme Cloud Services (Third-Party Sub-Processor)
CONTRACT ID: VND-2024-0891
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2024-06-01
EXPIRY DATE: 2026-05-31
ANNUAL CONTRACT VALUE: $2.4 Million
RELATIONSHIP: CRITICAL Vendor — Primary Kubernetes and compute infrastructure

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 1 — SERVICES AND UPTIME
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 Acme provides managed Kubernetes clusters, compute instances, and container registry services.

1.2 Uptime Guarantee: Acme guarantees 99.95% monthly uptime for all managed Kubernetes clusters.

1.3 SLA Penalties: If uptime falls below 99.95%, a 10% service credit applies. Below 99.9%, a 25% service credit applies. Below 99.0%, the organization may terminate the contract without penalty.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2 — DATA SOVEREIGNTY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 Acme is contractually prohibited from replicating or storing any of our data outside the regions we explicitly designate (US-East, EU-West).

2.2 Any unauthorized cross-region replication is a material breach triggering immediate contract termination rights and liability for resulting regulatory penalties.

2.3 Acme must maintain SOC2 Type II, ISO 27001, and ISO 27017 certifications at all times. Lapse of any certification is a material breach.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3 — SUBCONTRACTING AND SUB-PROCESSORS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Acme may NOT engage additional sub-processors without 30 days prior written notice and our explicit approval.

3.2 Any unauthorized sub-processing is a material breach triggering immediate contract termination rights.

3.3 Acme must maintain and share an up-to-date list of all sub-processors, their locations, and the data they access.

3.4 Sub-processors must meet the same security and compliance standards as Acme (SOC2, ISO 27001, GDPR-compliant DPA).

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4 — SECURITY AND ENCRYPTION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 All data stored on Acme infrastructure must be encrypted at rest using AES-256 with customer-managed encryption keys (CMEK).

4.2 Acme must support CMEK integration with our Cloud KMS. Google-managed encryption keys (GMEK) alone are NOT acceptable.

4.3 All data in transit between our systems and Acme infrastructure must use TLS 1.2 or higher.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5 — INCIDENT RESPONSE
═══════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 Acme must notify us of any confirmed security incident within 4 hours of detection.

5.2 For suspected data breaches, Acme must provide a detailed incident report within 72 hours.

5.3 Acme must cooperate with our incident response team and provide full access to relevant logs and forensic data.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 6 — AUDIT AND TERMINATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 Security Audit Rights: We retain the right to conduct annual security audits of Acme's infrastructure with 14 days advance notice.

6.2 Data Return: Upon contract termination, Acme must return all data in a portable format within 30 days.

6.3 Data Deletion: Acme must certify complete and irrecoverable deletion of all our data within 60 days of data return.

6.4 Post-termination audit rights: 12 months after termination.
