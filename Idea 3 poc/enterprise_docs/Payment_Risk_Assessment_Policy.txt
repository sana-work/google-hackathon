OWNER: CFO, Revenue Operations

1. PURPOSE
   Defines how payment behavior signals are interpreted for churn risk
   assessment and the appropriate financial interventions available.

2. PAYMENT RISK SIGNALS

   Signal                          | Risk Weight | Interpretation
   --------------------------------|-------------|-------------------------------------
   First late payment (>5 days)    | CRITICAL    | Strongest single churn predictor
   Payment method removal          | HIGH        | Customer preparing to leave
   Auto-renewal cancellation       | HIGH        | Explicit intent signal
   Downgrade request               | MEDIUM      | Budget pressure or value mismatch
   Invoice dispute filed           | MEDIUM      | Dissatisfaction with pricing/value
   Reduced purchase frequency      | MEDIUM      | Declining engagement
   Smaller order values            | LOW-MED     | Testing alternatives

3. FINANCIAL INTERVENTION OPTIONS (By Approval Level)

   3.1 CSM Authority (up to 10% of ACV):
       - Payment extension: up to 30 additional days
       - Monthly billing option (if currently annual)
       - One-time credit: up to 10% of last invoice

   3.2 Director Authority (up to 15% of ACV):
       - Loyalty discount: 15% off next renewal
       - Free add-on features for 90 days
       - Payment plan: split outstanding into 3 installments

   3.3 VP Authority (up to 20% of ACV):
       - Custom retention pricing for 12 months
       - Free tier upgrade for 6 months
       - Dedicated success engineer allocation

4. CONSTRAINTS
   4.1 Financial interventions MUST be documented with business justification
   4.2 Same customer cannot receive discounts in consecutive renewal cycles
   4.3 All offers above 10% ACV require written approval in CRM
   4.4 Lifetime discount cap: 25% of total contract value across all offers

5. PAYMENT MONITORING AUTOMATION
   - Daily scan of all accounts for payment delay signals
   - Auto-flag accounts with first-time late payment to retention system
   - Weekly report: Accounts with downgrade requests in pipeline
   - Alert: Any account >30 days past due auto-escalated to Collections + CSM
