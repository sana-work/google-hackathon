1 Problem Statement

Regulated industries face a relentless stream of regulatory changes. Compliance teams manually read hundreds of pages to assess which internal policies, SOPs, IT systems, and contracts are affected.

⚠ Risk: Missing a regulatory impact can result in ₹10Cr-₹500Cr+ penalties, license revocation, and reputational damage.
Metric Reality
Regulatory updates/year 300-500+ across jurisdictions
Pages per regulation 20-200+ pages
Time to assess (manual) 2-6 weeks per change
Cost of non-compliance ₹10Cr - ₹500Cr+

2 Solution

An AI system creating an automated regulatory nervous system — scanning, matching, and generating structured impact assessments.

  • Regulatory Monitoring: Gemini 1.5 Pro parses complex legal language and extracts actionable clauses
  • Enterprise Knowledge Graph: Semantic index of policies, SOPs, IT systems, data flows, vendor contracts with mapped dependencies
  • Impact Analysis: Severity-rated reports (Critical/High/Medium/Low) with affected assets and effort estimates
  • Remediation Tracker: Auto-generates tasks with owners, deadlines, and dependencies
  • Audit Trail: Regulation → Impact → Remediation → Completion evidence

4 Use Cases

📋 Example 1: Data Localization Circular

Regulation: All financial data must be stored within India within 180 days.

Impact: Cloud vendor contract (Singapore) — CRITICAL. Data Lake EU replication — CRITICAL. 3 policies need updates — HIGH.

Actions: Vendor renegotiation (Legal, 60d), Migration (IT, 120d), Policy update (Compliance, 30d)

📋 Example 2: Consent Management Update

Regulation: Explicit opt-in consent required for marketing communications.

Impact: Privacy policy uses opt-out — CRITICAL. CRM default "opted-in" — HIGH. Marketing SOP gap — HIGH.

Actions: Policy rewrite (Legal, 15d), CRM change (IT, 45d), SOP update (Marketing, 20d)
