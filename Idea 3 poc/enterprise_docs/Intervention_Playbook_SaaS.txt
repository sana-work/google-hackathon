1. OVERVIEW
   This playbook defines the specific intervention strategies for SaaS
   customers exhibiting silent disengagement patterns. All interventions
   must be personalized based on the customer's specific behavioral signals.

2. INTERVENTION MATRIX BY DISENGAGEMENT PATTERN

   PATTERN A: USAGE DECLINE ONLY
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Signals: Login frequency dropped >50%, feature depth declining
   Root Cause: Likely difficulty using product or lack of perceived value
   Intervention:
   - Send "What's New" digest highlighting features they haven't tried
   - Offer a 30-minute personalized onboarding refresher session
   - Share relevant case studies from their industry segment
   - If enterprise tier: Assign dedicated Customer Success Engineer

   PATTERN B: COMMUNICATION SILENCE
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Signals: Email open rate <10%, ignoring all notifications
   Root Cause: Content fatigue or email overload
   Intervention:
   - Switch to alternative channel (in-app notification, SMS)
   - Reduce email frequency to critical-only communications
   - Send a single high-value "We miss you" personalized message
   - Include an exclusive offer or early access to new feature

   PATTERN C: SUPPORT ABSENCE (SUSPICIOUS)
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Signals: No tickets filed despite known platform issues, no survey responses
   Root Cause: Customer has mentally disengaged; not worth the effort to complain
   Intervention:
   - Proactive outreach: "We noticed you might be experiencing [known issue]"
   - Offer immediate priority resolution for any open concerns
   - Share product roadmap to demonstrate commitment to improvement
   - Schedule executive-level relationship check-in

   PATTERN D: TRANSACTION DECAY
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Signals: Payment delays, usage downgrades, reduced spending
   Root Cause: Budget pressure or evaluating competitors
   Intervention:
   - Offer flexible payment terms or quarterly billing option
   - Present ROI analysis showing value delivered in last 12 months
   - If evaluating competitors: Schedule competitive comparison demo
   - Offer loyalty discount: 15% off next renewal (requires Director approval)

   PATTERN E: MULTI-CHANNEL DECLINE (CRITICAL)
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   Signals: All four channels showing simultaneous decline
   Root Cause: Complete disengagement; churn imminent within 15-30 days
   Intervention:
   - IMMEDIATE manager escalation with full context brief
   - CEO/VP-level personal outreach within 12 hours
   - Offer: Custom retention package (up to 20% of ACV)
   - Schedule on-site or virtual executive business review
   - Document all interactions in CRM with escalation tag

3. PERSONALIZED MESSAGE TEMPLATES

   3.1 Usage Decline Template:
   "Hi [Name], I noticed you haven't had a chance to explore [Feature X]
   yet. Customers in [their industry] have seen [specific benefit] from it.
   Would you like a quick 15-minute walkthrough? I'd love to help you
   get the most out of your subscription."

   3.2 Win-back Template:
   "Hi [Name], we value your partnership and wanted to check in. We've
   made significant improvements to [area they last engaged with],
   including [specific update]. Can we schedule a brief call to discuss
   how we can better support your goals?"

4. INTERVENTION SUCCESS METRICS
   - Re-engagement Rate: Target >45% for LOW risk, >30% for MEDIUM
   - Time-to-Response: <48h for LOW, <24h for MEDIUM, <12h for HIGH
   - Retention Rate: 85% of intervened customers retained at 90-day mark
   - NPS Improvement: +15 points post-intervention (measured at 30 days)

5. ESCALATION PATH
   Level 1: Automated email/in-app → CSM
   Level 2: CSM outreach → Customer Success Director
   Level 3: Director escalation → VP/Account Executive
   Level 4: Executive sponsor → C-level intervention
