1. PURPOSE
   Defines the KPIs, benchmarks, and measurement methodology for
   evaluating the effectiveness of retention interventions triggered
   by the Silent Escalation Predictor.

2. PRIMARY KPIs

   KPI                          | Definition                         | Target
   -----------------------------|------------------------------------|---------  
   Intervention Success Rate    | % of at-risk customers retained    | >40%
   Time-to-Intervention (TTI)   | Hours from risk detection to first | <24h
                                | customer contact                   |
   Net Revenue Retention (NRR)  | Revenue retained vs. expected      | >110%
   Customer Health Score (CHS)  | Post-intervention engagement level | >60/100
   Save Cost Efficiency         | Cost of intervention / saved ACV   | <10%

3. INTERVENTION EFFECTIVENESS BY TYPE (Benchmark Data)

   Type                     | Success Rate | Avg Cost | Time to Effect
   -------------------------|-------------|----------|----------------
   Automated Email          | 33%         | ₹200     | 7-14 days
   In-App Notification      | 28%         | ₹50      | 3-7 days
   CSM Phone Outreach       | 44%         | ₹2,500   | 1-3 days
   Manager Video Call       | 54%         | ₹8,000   | 1-2 days
   Executive Intervention   | 75%         | ₹25,000  | Same day
   Loyalty Discount Offer   | 38%         | Variable | 7-14 days
   Feature Demo/Training    | 42%         | ₹5,000   | 3-7 days

4. MEASUREMENT WINDOWS
   4.1 Immediate Effect: Customer engagement within 7 days of intervention
   4.2 Short-term Retention: Customer active at 30-day mark
   4.3 Sustained Retention: Customer active at 90-day mark (primary KPI)
   4.4 Long-term Health: Customer engagement level at 180-day mark
   4.5 Renewal Outcome: Did customer renew at next renewal cycle?

5. FEEDBACK LOOP METRICS
   5.1 Model Accuracy: % of HIGH RISK customers who actually churned
       (without intervention) — measured via holdout group
       Current accuracy: 78% (target: >85%)
   5.2 False Positive Rate: % flagged as HIGH RISK who were actually fine
       Current rate: 12% (target: <10%)
   5.3 False Negative Rate: % who churned without being flagged
       Current rate: 22% (target: <15%)

6. A/B TESTING FRAMEWORK
   6.1 All new intervention strategies must be A/B tested before rollout
   6.2 Minimum sample: 100 customers per variant
   6.3 Minimum duration: 30 days
   6.4 Statistical significance: p < 0.05
   6.5 Primary metric: 90-day retention rate
   6.6 Guard rails: Monitor for adverse effects on customer satisfaction

7. REPORTING CADENCE
   - Daily: Intervention pipeline dashboard (automated)
   - Weekly: CSM team performance review
   - Monthly: Executive retention board
   - Quarterly: Model retraining and calibration report
   - Annually: Strategic retention program review
