1. ABOUT GLOBEX CORPORATION
────────────────────────────
Globex Corporation is a global technology services company headquartered in Bangalore, India, with offices in 15 countries. Founded in 2003, Globex employs over 25,000 professionals serving clients across BFSI, healthcare, telecom, and manufacturing sectors.

Mission: "To accelerate digital transformation through responsible innovation."
Core Values: Integrity, Innovation, Inclusion, Impact.

2. CODE OF CONDUCT
───────────────────
2.1 Employees must act with honesty, integrity, and professionalism in all business interactions.
2.2 Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to the Ethics Committee within 7 business days.
2.3 Acceptance of gifts or hospitality from clients/vendors exceeding INR 5,000 must be declared and approved by the Department Head.
2.4 Political activity during work hours or using company resources is prohibited.
2.5 All employees must complete the annual Ethics & Compliance e-learning module by March 31st each year.

3. WORKING HOURS & FLEXIBILITY
───────────────────────────────
3.1 Standard working hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM (local time), Monday to Friday.
3.2 Flexible Work Policy: Employees in eligible roles may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
3.3 Core collaboration hours: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM — all employees must be available during this window for meetings and synchronous communication.
3.4 Overtime: Non-exempt employees working beyond 45 hours/week are entitled to overtime compensation at 1.5x the hourly rate.

4. DRESS CODE
──────────────
4.1 Business Professional: Required for client-facing meetings, Board presentations, and external events.
4.2 Business Casual: Acceptable for regular office days.
4.3 Casual Friday: Permitted on Fridays unless client meetings are scheduled.
4.4 Safety gear (PPE) is mandatory in data center, lab, and manufacturing areas.

5. PERFORMANCE MANAGEMENT
──────────────────────────
5.1 Performance reviews are conducted bi-annually: Mid-Year (July) and Year-End (January).
5.2 Rating Scale: Exceptional (5), Exceeds (4), Meets (3), Developing (2), Below (1).
5.3 Employees rated "Below" (1) for two consecutive cycles are placed on a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) for 90 days.
5.4 Annual increments are determined by the Compensation Committee based on individual ratings, band position, and business performance.

6. LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT
──────────────────────────
6.1 Each employee has an annual Learning Budget of INR 50,000 for certifications, courses, and conferences.
6.2 Mandatory certifications by role are tracked in the Learning Management System (LMS).
6.3 Employees pursuing higher education (MBA, M.Tech) may apply for the Globex Education Assistance Program covering up to 50% of tuition fees (max INR 5,00,000).

7. ANTI-HARASSMENT & EQUAL OPPORTUNITY
───────────────────────────────────────
7.1 Globex maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards harassment, discrimination, and bullying.
7.2 Complaints can be filed through the POSH Committee (Prevention of Sexual Harassment) via ethics@globex-corp.com.
7.3 All complaints are investigated within 30 business days. Retaliation against complainants is a terminable offense.
7.4 Globex is an equal opportunity employer. Hiring, promotion, and compensation decisions are based solely on merit, qualifications, and performance.

8. SEPARATION & EXIT
─────────────────────
8.1 Notice Period: 60 days for Band 4 and above; 30 days for Band 3 and below.
8.2 Exit interviews are mandatory and conducted by HR Business Partners.
8.3 All company property (laptop, ID card, access tokens) must be returned on the last working day.
8.4 Final settlement is processed within 45 days of the last working day.
8.5 Non-compete clause: Employees in leadership roles (Band 6+) have a 12-month non-compete period for direct competitors.
