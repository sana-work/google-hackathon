Section 01
Executive Summary 20% Weight

🛑 The Organizational Problem: Assessing new regulatory changes requires compliance teams to manually read hundreds of pages to locate which specific internal IT policies, SLA thresholds, and Cloud architectures require change. The sheer manual effort causes delayed compliance reporting and introduces human error on penalty-driving regulations.

💡 Our Solution: Regulatory Radar acts as a proactive multi-agent auditor. Using Vertex AI to map regulatory amendments to an internal Google Drive Knowledge connector indexing past internal documentation, it actively maps out internal policies requiring updates and flags potential gaps autonomously in seconds.
