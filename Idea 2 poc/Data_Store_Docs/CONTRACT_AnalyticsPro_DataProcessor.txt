CONTRACT: Data Analytics and Business Intelligence Services Agreement
VENDOR: AnalyticsPro International Pte. Ltd.
CONTRACT ID: VND-2024-0587
EFFECTIVE DATE: 2024-08-01
EXPIRY DATE: 2026-07-31
ANNUAL CONTRACT VALUE: ₹1.8 Crore
RELATIONSHIP: HIGH Vendor — Analytics and BI platform provider

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 1 — SERVICES PROVIDED
═══════════════════════════════════════════

1.1 AnalyticsPro provides a managed business intelligence platform, including dashboarding, reporting, customer segmentation analytics, and predictive analytics APIs.

1.2 The organization sends customer behavioral data (browsing patterns, purchase history, product interactions) to AnalyticsPro for processing and visualization.

1.3 AnalyticsPro also provides a customer segmentation ML model that processes customer demographic and behavioral data to generate audience segments for marketing campaigns.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 2 — DATA CENTER LOCATIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

2.1 AnalyticsPro's primary infrastructure is hosted in Singapore (AWS ap-southeast-1).
2.2 AnalyticsPro does NOT have data centers in India or the European Union.
2.3 Disaster Recovery: AnalyticsPro uses AWS Sydney (ap-southeast-2) for disaster recovery.

2.4 Data Residency: All organization data sent to AnalyticsPro is processed and stored in Singapore. There is no data localization clause in the current contract.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 3 — DATA HANDLING
═══════════════════════════════════════════

3.1 Data Received: AnalyticsPro receives the following data categories from the organization:
    (a) Customer pseudonymized IDs (linked to CRM records)
    (b) Behavioral data: page views, click events, purchase transactions, search queries
    (c) Demographic data: age range, gender, location (city-level)
    (d) Product interaction data: viewed products, cart additions, wishlists

3.2 PII Handling: The organization provides pseudonymized customer IDs to AnalyticsPro. However, the behavioral data combined with demographic data may constitute personal data under GDPR and DPDPA definitions (re-identification risk).

3.3 Data Retention: AnalyticsPro retains all received data for the duration of the contract plus 6 months for transition purposes. No automatic deletion upon contract expiry.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 4 — SECURITY
═══════════════════════════════════════════

4.1 AnalyticsPro maintains SOC2 Type II and ISO 27001 certifications.
4.2 Encryption at rest: AES-256 with AnalyticsPro-managed keys.
4.3 Encryption in transit: TLS 1.2.
4.4 Access to organization data is restricted to AnalyticsPro employees with a business need.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 5 — BREACH NOTIFICATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════

5.1 AnalyticsPro will notify the organization of any suspected data breach within 48 hours of detection.
5.2 AnalyticsPro will provide root cause analysis within 30 days.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 6 — SUB-PROCESSORS
═══════════════════════════════════════════

6.1 AnalyticsPro uses the following sub-processors:
    (a) AWS (Singapore, Sydney) — Infrastructure hosting
    (b) DataRefinery Inc. (US, Oregon) — ML model training pipeline
        — NOTE: Customer data may be processed in the US by DataRefinery for model training purposes

6.2 AnalyticsPro will notify the organization 14 days before engaging new sub-processors.

═══════════════════════════════════════════
SECTION 7 — COMPLIANCE GAPS (Internal Assessment Notes)
═══════════════════════════════════════════

7.1 Transfer Impact Assessment (TIA) for Singapore transfer: Completed in 2024. Due for annual review.
7.2 Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs): SCCs are in place for EU data but use the 2021 version — not yet updated to incorporate the new Module 5 requirements.
7.3 DPDPA Compliance: Singapore is on the DPDPA approved jurisdiction list, BUT the sub-processor DataRefinery in the US may not be compliant as the US is not on the approved list.
7.4 No data localization clause exists — financial data flowing to AnalyticsPro (via behavioral analytics on payment transactions) may violate RBI data localization requirements.
